# Crystal Growth AI Predictor

## Overview

This is an AI-powered system that understands protein data and helps researchers make better decisions about crystal growth.

It also includes a natural language interface, so users can simply ask questions instead of needing deep technical knowledge.

---

## Inspiration

This project is inspired by protein crystal growth experiments conducted in space, especially on the International Space Station (ISS).

In microgravity, proteins can form better crystals, helping scientists study them more clearly. This made me think: what if AI could help predict and understand crystal growth faster, both on Earth and in space?

---

## The Problem

Protein crystal growth is slow and difficult.

Scientists often spend a lot of time testing different conditions, and even then, results are not guaranteed. This becomes even more important in space research, where time and resources are limited.

---

## The Solution

This tool helps make that process faster and easier.

It can:

* Predict the best conditions for growing protein crystals
* Analyze protein structures even when full data is not available
* Answer general questions about proteins in a simple way

---

## How It Works

* It reads protein sequences and structural data
* If full structure data (PDB) is available, it uses that
* If not, it predicts the structure using AI
* It analyzes patterns and suggests crystal growth conditions
* You can also ask it general questions about proteins, not just predictions

---

## Why This Matters

This tool can help:

* Save time in protein research
* Support space biology experiments
* Make complex protein data easier to understand
